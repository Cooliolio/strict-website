ABOUT STRICT

The Goal

The goal is to satisfy customers with their needs. This could be a flyer, logo, shirt design but also a website, application or just help with their solutions. It's all about creating the art for the customers needs.

The Name

The name 'Strict' comes from Yasin's surname, Yavuz, meaning strict and serious. Most of the things we make are following this meaning. The slogan, 'Strictly more' , is a paradox. Because how can you have strictly something but also more?

The Story

Strict was founded by Yasin Yavuz who has had a great passion for everything digital since a young age. Making websites, drawing in msPaint, building the computer itself, ... His hobbies and passions became his job.
